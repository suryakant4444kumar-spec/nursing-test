 ✓
C. History of vaginal delivery
D. Having unprotected sex
💡
Explanation
Clients under age 35 have a non-modifiable risk factor for PID because younger clients are statistically more likely to have new or multiple sexual partners, increasing their risk of infection. Other options refer to modifiable or irrelevant factors.
