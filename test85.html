A. Neck
B. Jaw ✓

D. Legs
💡
Explanation
Rigor mortis, the post-mortem stiffening of muscles, typically begins in the smaller muscles of the body and progresses to the larger muscles. The first muscles affected are generally those in the jaw, particularly the muscles of the face and neck.

A. Virginia Henderson

Question 3 of 50
Which of the following is the responsibilities of the staff nurse?
A. Quality care
B. Observation & reporting
C. Record keeping
D. All of the above ✓
💡
Explanation

  • Quality Care: Staff nurses are responsible for providing high-quality patient care. This includes ensuring that care meets established standards and is delivered safely and effectively.

  • Observation & Reporting: Staff nurses are tasked with closely observing patients' conditions, noting any changes, and reporting these observations to the healthcare team. This helps in timely intervention and management of patient care.

  • Record Keeping: Accurate and thorough documentation is a critical responsibility of staff nurses. This includes recording patient assessments, interventions, responses to treatment, and other relevant information in medical records.
