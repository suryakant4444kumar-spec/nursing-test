After teaching the client with severe rheumatoid arthritis about prescribed methotrexate (Rheumatrex), which of the following statements indicates the need for further teaching?

B. “I must not drink any alcohol while I’m taking this drug.”
C. “I should brush my teeth after every meal.”
D. “I will continue taking my birth control pills.”
💡
Explanation
Methotrexate (Rheumatrex) is a disease-modifying antirheumatic drug (DMARD) commonly used to treat rheumatoid arthritis. However, taking large doses of folic acid without a doctor’s guidance can reduce methotrexate’s effectiveness. Therefore, the client should not take vitamins, particularly folic acid, without consulting their healthcare provider first

C. Pain management and joint protection ✓

💡

  • Pain relief with analgesics or NSAIDs

  • Physical therapy and exercise

  • Joint protection strategies

  • Sometimes, lifestyle changes like weight management

Immunosuppressive and antirheumatic drugs are more relevant to inflammatory arthritis (like rheumatoid arthritis), and antibiotics are for infections, not osteoarthritis.
